Terms of Service

Please, read it carefully. Keep in mind that the TOS can change at anytime and without any advice.

  1. GENERAL TERMS
    1. Inquiring a commission, you will be following the proposed terms of service below. Terms can change or be added at any time.
    2. Please, be kind and patient. I will treat you with all respect, and I want to be treated with all due respect too. If your behavior in any way makes me uncomfortable or embarrassed, your commission may be cancelled.
    3. In case you do not follow the rules of terms, your commission may be cancelled.

  2. ORDERS AND PAYMENT
    1. Via discord: @/tozeos or via Ko-fi: ko-fi.com/tozeos .
    2. I may or may not accept the order for reasons such as complexity, time constraints, etc. I have the right to refuse the commission without further explanation.
    3. The customer must send the character(s) and background details/references of the art as clear as possible, the more information they give about expressions, poses, physical characteristics, and personality, the better. If by any chance the customer requires changes after the final design is done, an extra percentage will be charged or even the changes might not be done.
    4. In case the client wants the artist to create a character without any references to images or illustrations, only based on the information given in text, an extra fee will be charged depending on the complexity of the art.
    5. Payment must be made through PayPal, in USD (United States Dollars), via PIX, via invoice or by sending me your email so that I can make the payment request. If payment is made via payment slip, it's mandatory to send the payment voucher, or else I must wait until the receipt of the money in my bank account to continue the commission work.

  3. PRODUCTION
    1. When the order is placed, a draft will be made according to the customer's specifications. When finalized, it will be shown for approval or denial. If approved, the payment is required to continue.
    2. Once payment is made, I will start working your piece straight away. The deadline for finalizing the art may vary according to the complexity of the work and the time available for drawing, so keep that in mind.
    3. During the ending of the creation process, I will constantly show it for you to analyze and be aware of any changes. However, you can see the artwork at any time during the process if you request it in advance.
    4. You can request changes to the artwork before the finalization and painting step. Once finalized, if you request an extra change, there will be a charge or even the changes may not be carried out depending on the complexity.

  4. REFUND
    1. Before making any payment, make sure that you will not regret having requested the service, as I will not make a refund after payment.

  5. DELIVERY
    1. After the art is done it will be sent to the customer's email in high-definition quality which may be in PNG or GIF file format, according to the customer preferences.
    2. The orders will be made and delivered following the order and payment queue, which means that the customer who ordered and paid first must receive them first.
